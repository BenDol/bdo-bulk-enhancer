Bonfyre's BDO Bulk Enhancer

Get an idea of how many attempts it will take you to enhance your items. Put in the enhancement chance, how many player iterations and how many items each player will be successfully enhancing. The higher the numbers the slower the calculation, but the result will be a more accurate. A convenience tool for figuring out your enhancement rate, made by Bonfyre!
